This portfolio site serves as my professional presence for healthcare providers seeking virtual assistance. It highlights:

- **Services Offered**: Appointment scheduling, medical coding & billing, remote patient monitoring, care coordination, insurance verification, telehealth support, and medical scribing
- **Credentials**: Pharmacy background, HIPAA compliance certification, Medical VA training
- **Technical Proficiency**: Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth, Zoom, Slack, and other healthcare tools
- **Testimonials**: Client feedback demonstrating expertise
