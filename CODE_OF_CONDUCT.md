# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project and everyone participating in it is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and professional environment for all. We are dedicated to fostering a space where healthcare professionals, colleagues, and collaborators can interact respectfully and productively.

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior That Contributes to a Positive Environment

✅ Using welcoming and inclusive language  
✅ Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences  
✅ Gracefully accepting constructive criticism  
✅ Focusing on what is best for the healthcare community  
✅ Showing empathy towards other community members  
✅ Maintaining professional communication standards  
✅ Respecting patient privacy and HIPAA compliance  
✅ Supporting continuous learning and improvement  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

❌ Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation based on any characteristic  
❌ Offensive comments or conduct related to personal attributes  
❌ Non-consensual sharing of private information  
❌ Any behavior that violates HIPAA or healthcare privacy standards  
❌ Spamming, advertising, or self-promotion in inappropriate contexts  
❌ Disruptive or unprofessional communication  
❌ Sharing confidential healthcare information  
❌ Conduct that could compromise patient safety or privacy  

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All GitHub discussions and comments
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Issues and discussions
- Any public interaction related to this project
- Professional communications about healthcare services

---

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action regarding unacceptable behavior
- Protecting the confidentiality of report sources
- Maintaining a safe and welcoming community

---

## Privacy & Healthcare Standards

### HIPAA Compliance
- Never share identifiable patient information
- Protect confidential healthcare data
- Respect privacy regulations
- Follow healthcare industry standards

### Professional Ethics
- Maintain confidentiality of client information
- Use information only for intended purposes
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Practice integrity in all interactions
